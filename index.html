My Work

Thyme Savers

Ever been in too much of a rush to cook? Thyme Savers is the perfect solution for you. providing users with quick and simple one pot recipes that come from the ingredients you already have in your closet. Simply upload a list of what you have or filter through the ingredients you want to cook with and you'll have a mreal ready in 15 minutes or less. And then you're out the door refueled and ready to tackle your busy schedule!

NBA.com Redesign

Being one of the most profitable sports organizations in the world you would think that the NBA's website would be easily accessible to the organizations large fan and consumer base. However, the website is extremely cluttered with no clear visual hierarchy. It's hard to find the information you're looking for through the multitude of tabs and there's not even a search bar. My team and I tackled this redesign with the goal of improving on the UI of the website to put user's needs first as well as making the website more workable so the NBA can profit off it better too.
